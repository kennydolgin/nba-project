Quick term guide
Player-season
One player on one team in one NBA season.
Comparable-season median
Median team win percentage among the nearest 40 similar statistical profiles.
Role breadth
Count of strong role dimensions in that season.
Defensive events
Steals + blocks; useful box-score events, not all defense.

A player-season is one player on one team in one NBA season. Role labels describe that season's statistical profile, not overall player quality or permanent identity.

Role map

Player-seasons by scoring load and role breadth

Each point is one player-season. Horizontal position shows scoring load; vertical position shows non-scoring contribution. Color shows team win context. Hover for exact values.

Role breadth means the number of strong role areas. eFG% is shooting efficiency accounting for 3-pointers. Per 36 means a rate normalized to 36 minutes.
